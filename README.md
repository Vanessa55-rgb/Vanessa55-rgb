## ✨ About Me

Developer focused on **applied AI** and **backend engineering**, building solutions that automate processes and improve how organizations work. Currently studying Software Engineering and a graduate of an intensive development bootcamp at **RIWI**, where I strengthened my skills in **Python, C#, ASP.NET, and SQL**.

I work at the intersection of backend development, data management, API integration, and process automation — using these as the foundation for scalable, functional systems.

Right now I work as a **Context Engineer** on an AI project, where I structure, organize, and optimize the knowledge that feeds AI systems: preparing technical documentation, standardizing information for knowledge bases, and organizing data for **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)** systems so it's clear, consistent, and usable by AI agents.

What drives me is solving problems, learning new technologies, and building solutions with real impact on operational efficiency.
